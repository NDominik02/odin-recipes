Spaghetti recipe

Description

Take the meat, cook it then wait for the pasta. :)

Put the pasta in boiling water for 10 min.

Done.

Ingredients

  • 500g pasta
  • 500g beef mince
  • cheese
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • some salt
  • Sauce

Steps

  1. Cook the meat
  2. Cook the pasta
  3. Cheese on the top of it
  4. Enjoy
